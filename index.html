A propos

As we explore text manipulation, this project focuses on how changing the structure, order, and number of words can alter the meaning.

Specifically, we emphasize the impact on poems. While prose relies on the overall structure, moving the position of each word separately can completely change the intention of the poem as it is read differently.

This approach not only modifies the intention of the poem but also provides an opportunity to interpret the meaning in a different way.
